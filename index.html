Common Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, our support team is always ready to help.

How does AsraCloud achieve such low latency in India?

We operate our own infrastructure in Mumbai with direct peering to major Indian ISPs. This eliminates unnecessary hops and ensures the shortest possible path between your players and your game server, resulting in ping times often under 10ms across India.

What makes your hardware different from competitors?

We exclusively use AMD Ryzen 9 7950X processors with DDR5 RAM and PCIe 4.0 NVMe storage. This next-gen hardware delivers up to 40% better performance than the older hardware used by many competitors, especially for gaming workloads.

Can I switch between plans as my needs change?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your control panel. We prorate the difference, so you only pay for what you use. Most plan changes take effect within minutes without any downtime.

Do you offer custom solutions for large communities?

Yes, we specialize in custom solutions for large gaming communities and esports organizations. Our enterprise plans can include dedicated hardware, custom network configurations, and priority support. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.

How do you handle DDoS attacks?

Our network is protected by enterprise-grade DDoS mitigation capable of handling attacks exceeding 1Tbps. We use a combination of scrubbing centers, behavioral analysis, and custom rules to ensure your game stays online even during massive attacks.
